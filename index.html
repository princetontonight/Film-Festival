WELCOME TO THE INAUGURAL PRINCETON UNIVERSITY FILM FESTIVAL

November 11, 2017

The 2017 Film Festival aims to bridge the gap between students and professionals in the entertainment industry. The all-day event will feature keynote speeches, workshops, Q&As, screenings of students' work, and panels with some of the most prolific entertainment professionals in the film industry.

In its inaugural year, PUFF aims to create an intimate setting that connects top college students with experts and companies in entertainment, exposes high school students to their college options, and provides a fun and engaging atmosphere for its guests to enjoy everything that entertainment has to offer.
